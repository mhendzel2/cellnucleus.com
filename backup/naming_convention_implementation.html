Guidelines for Future Content Creation

Naming Checklist

  • Use lowercase letters only
  • Replace spaces with underscores
  • Include topic, type, and status
  • Avoid special characters
  • Keep names descriptive but concise

Content Standards

  • Maintain consistent visual template
  • Include comprehensive scientific figures
  • Follow established section structure
  • Provide proper citations and references
  • Ensure mobile-responsive design
