Risk Assessment & Mitigation

High Risk: Content Migration Complexity

High Impact

25 complex HTML files with embedded images and scientific notation require careful migration to preserve formatting and functionality.

Mitigation: Implement automated content parsing with manual quality assurance checks. Create backup migration strategy and staging environment for testing.

Medium Risk: Performance with Rich Media

Medium Impact

Large scientific images and interactive 3D models may impact page load times, especially on mobile devices.

Mitigation: Implement progressive image loading, WebP format conversion, and CDN optimization. Design mobile-first with optional high-resolution content.

Low Risk: User Adoption

Low Impact

Scientific community may be slow to adopt new digital tools and interfaces.

Mitigation: Gradual rollout with user feedback integration. Maintain familiar navigation patterns and provide comprehensive user documentation.
