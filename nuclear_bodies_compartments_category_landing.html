Category Overview

Introduction

Nuclear bodies represent dynamic, membraneless organelles that compartmentalize specific nuclear functions through phase separation and protein-protein interactions. This collection of 12 comprehensive reviews examines the structure, function, and regulation of major nuclear compartments.

These specialized domains organize essential cellular processes including RNA processing, ribosome biogenesis, DNA repair, and transcriptional regulation through sophisticated molecular mechanisms.
