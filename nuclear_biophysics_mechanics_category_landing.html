Introduction

Nuclear biophysics and mechanics encompass the physical properties, mechanical behavior, and quantitative analysis of nuclear organization and function. This collection of 6 comprehensive reviews explores the biophysical principles governing nuclear structure, dynamics, and responses to mechanical forces within the cellular environment.
